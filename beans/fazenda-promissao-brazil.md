## Notes

Classic Brazilian natural from Cerrado Mineiro. Cacao nibs, chocolate, cherry, hazelnut
finish. Medium acidity, silky body. Very dense puck — required much coarser grind than
expected for espresso (grind 19 vs typical 13).

Tagged as everyday espresso: consistent, approachable, no fuss once dialed.
