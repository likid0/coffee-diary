## Notes

Swiss Water Decaf from Chiapas. Label: spices, marzipan, chocolate.
Swiss Water process = no chemicals, just water and carbon filters. Gentler than Sugar Cane
process — softer, cleaner cup but less body.

## vs Nomad D.CO.DCF (Sugar Cane Decaf)

Swiss Water bean structure is less porous than Sugar Cane → may need slightly finer grind.
Start at grind 11 (vs Nomad decaf grind 10 — go finer because less porous, not coarser).
Expect softer body and cleaner profile vs Nomad's richer mouthfeel.

## Starting parameters (espresso)

Grind: 11
Dose: 20g in / 40g out / 27–30s
Open from: 2026-05-29 (day 8)
