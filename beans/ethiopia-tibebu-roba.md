## Notes

Premium Ethiopian natural at 2,000m — wild-growing Kurume and Wolisho heirlooms.
Pineapple, cherry, red fruits, bright acidity, silky mouthfeel, intense sweetness.
Espresso-roasted specifically. Very high altitude = very dense bean.

Expect to go finer than Brazilian naturals — start grind 13, move to 14 only if it chokes.
Brewed on Barista Express Impress.
