## Notes

High-scoring washed Bourbon from Rusizi. Fruit, chocolate, caramel. Omni roast —
works for both V60 and espresso. Complex enough that dialing in both methods is worthwhile.

Complex at filter: clean cup with frutal/chocolatado/caramelo profile.
Challenging for espresso — fruit upfront but sour finish at coarser grinds.
