## Notes

Kenyan AA from Baragwi Coop. Redcurrant, molasses, blueberry, chocolate, mandarin.
SL28/SL34 fruit complexity demands freshness — compounds degrade rapidly past 30 days.

## Age Warning

Use within 14-30 days post-roast for full fruit expression.
Past 40 days: aromatic compounds are gone. No grind or temperature adjustment recovers
lost aromatics — age is the hard limit for this variety.
