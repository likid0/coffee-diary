## Tasting

Grind dropped from 4 to 3. Saltiness now only detectable at the back of the
palate, no longer dominant. As straight espresso saltiness was partially masked
but still present. As Americano (44g + 110ml hot water) — decent cup, saltiness
receding, some sweetness starting to show.

Day 16 from roast — right in predicted peak window (14-16 days).

## Progression

- Grind 9: salty + acidic, fast pour
- Grind 4 + 15s pre-infusion + 44g out: saltiness mostly gone, no sweetness, flat
- Grind 3 + 15s pre-infusion + 44g out: saltiness background only, sweetness emerging ✓
