## Tasting

Flat, no fruit, generic coffee. No redcurrant, no blueberry, no mandarin.
Two attempts at different temperatures and grinds — same result both times.

## Dial-in path

- Grind 60, 93°C — flat, no fruit ❌
- Grind 61, 92°C — no improvement, same flat result ❌

## Conclusion

Bean too old at 46+ days post-roast. Aromatic compounds fully degraded.
No grind or temperature adjustment can recover lost aromatics — age is the hard limit
for SL28/SL34. The bag is finished.

## Lesson

Use Kenyan washed varieties within 14-30 days post-roast for full fruit expression.
