## Tasting

Peach-tea aromatics disappear completely. Shifts to traditional balanced coffee
flavor. Reduced acidity and sourness. Heavier body. Fermented fruit character gets
buried under structure. Not the intended profile for this bean.

## Next

Hypothesis: more coffee mass extracts more structure which overwhelms the delicate
fruit. To recover fruit at 18g:
- Coarser grind: +2-3 clicks (66-69)
- Lower temp: 88-89°C
- Goal: reduce extraction slightly to let the fruit back through
