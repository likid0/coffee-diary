## Tasting

Incredible fruitiness and acidity. Mango forward, bright, clean. Best cup of this bean so far.

## Technique (no-bloom 50/50)

1. No bloom — pour 150g directly, switch open
2. Wait 45–50s
3. Close switch, pour remaining 150g to 300g (~1:15)
4. Immersion until 2:00–2:05
5. Open switch → drawdown finishes 2:45–2:50

## Why this works

Skipping the bloom is unconventional but suits this bean: washed anaerobic process means
CO2 outgassing is less aggressive than a natural, so bloom adds little value.
The large 150g first pour saturates the bed evenly without channeling.
Shorter immersion (2:00 vs 2:30) keeps the extraction clean and preserves the bright
acidity without going heavy or muddy — perfect for the Wush Wush's exotic fruit character.

## Dial-in path

- V60, 64 clicks, 92°C (day 9) — loads of fruit, slight sourness when hot ⚠️
- V60, 64 clicks, 92°C (day 13) — consistent, same sourness ⚠️
- Switch no-bloom, 62 clicks, 92°C (day 13) — incredible fruitiness and acidity ✅
